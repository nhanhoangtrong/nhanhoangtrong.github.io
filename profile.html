bio

I'm a mobile application and website developer. I graduated with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Ho Chi Minh University of Science. Currently, I work as a freelancer at home. Design is one of my hobby. Most of the skills that I have come from self-taught. I also archived a lot of knowledge about data structures, algorithms, designing patterns and machine learning from school.

After graduated I had worked for a Japan company for two months. Then I decided to quit the job and became a freelancer. I have worked with many individual clients and companies. I find my self more challenge in designing and developing products from scratch. That how I improve my skills and acquire more experience.

Although I've learned English from middle school until now, my writing and speaking skills never have been advanced. So I try to listen to UK-US musics and read English articles from Internet. I hope some day I can speak English fluently.
